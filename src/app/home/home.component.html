Welcome to Bar Manager

This application is designed to help Home Bar enthusiasts manage their home bar and use ingredients to manage 'makeable' recipes.

Bar Manager also includes a Liquor DB to help amatuer mixologists learn more about Liquors and how they are made, where they are from and other information to help you become more informed.

Usage

Use the pop-out menu in the top left corner to navigate to a particular page and go from there.
